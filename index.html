Hello,

My name is Nahyan Khan, and I am a software engineering undergraduate at the University of Sheffield.

This website was made as an assignment for the COM1008 module as part of my course - this homepage contains some personal details about myself.

I picked the course because I would like to work in a programming environment. I particularly am interested in programming computer games, and have made several small projects myself. I intend to learn the Godot engine in my own time to be able to further my skillset.

The university experience that I've had so far has been by and large a great time! Getting used to living alone has been rather overwhelming, and learning to cook has been the most difficult undertaking so far. However, I thoroughly enjoy living in the city, with shops, bars and social events just a stones throw away.

My main hobby is music - specifically, playing and composing. I am classically taught on the piano, grade 8, but I have also self taught myself drums, and am the drummer of a heavy metal band, Shed Dust, started by myself and my friend.

Finally, I also make paid commissions for charts on a game called Clone Hero, though I rarely receive requests and when I do get one it doesn't earn me much.
